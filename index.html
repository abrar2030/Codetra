Looking ahead, I aim to advance quantitative finance by embedding advanced machine‑learning into trading strategies, building real‑time risk‑management frameworks and exploring blockchain for transparent markets. My goal is to deliver resilient, data‑driven solutions that drive measurable impact at the intersection of AI and finance.
